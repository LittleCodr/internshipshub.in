## Role overview

You will steer user discovery, draft product requirement documents, and collaborate with design and engineering to ship improvements across Zoho's CRM and Finance products.

## Responsibilities

- Own product metrics for a defined module and track releases.
- Translate customer feedback into actionable product requirements.
- Partner with design to create wireframes and prototypes.
- Coordinate with engineering to execute sprints.

## Ideal candidate profile

- Demonstrated product thinking via internships, hackathons, or side projects.
- Strong analytical abilities using SQL or product analytics tools.
- Excellent written and verbal communication.

## Hiring process

1. Online application with product case submission.
2. Product thinking assessment and business writing test.
3. Final onsite rounds with product leaders.
