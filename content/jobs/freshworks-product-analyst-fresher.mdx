## What you will work on

- Track product activation metrics and user funnels across Freshdesk, Freshservice, and Freshsales.
- Collaborate with engineers and PMs to instrument events and build dashboards that surface adoption signals.
- Run experiments on onboarding flows and pricing pages to improve conversion.

## What makes you a fit

1. Hands-on experience with SQL, spreadsheets, or BI tools through internships or university projects.
2. Comfort interpreting large data sets and presenting clear narratives to business stakeholders.
3. Excellent communication skills and curiosity to explore customer behavior.

## Interview stages

- Online analytics challenge covering SQL and business reasoning.
- Virtual panel interview with product managers and data leaders.
- Culture add conversation with the Freshworks people team.

Employees enjoy hybrid flexibility in Chennai with medical insurance, learning budgets, and mentorship across product pods.
