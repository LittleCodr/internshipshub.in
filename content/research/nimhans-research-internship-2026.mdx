# NIMHANS Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
NIMHANS offers internships in neuroscience, psychiatry, neuroimaging, and digital health. This guide summarizes 2026 eligibility, stipend reality, and application steps (Expected/Indicative timelines).

## Program Overview
6–8 week on-site projects with faculty clinicians/researchers. Work includes data collection, analysis, literature reviews, or tool development. Some labs pay stipends; many provide certificates.

## Domains Offered
- Cognitive neuroscience and neuroimaging
- Clinical psychology and psychiatry studies
- Neurobiology and molecular labs
- Digital health/EEG/BCI analytics

## Eligibility
- Relevant degree track; ethics compliance
- SOP aligned to lab; prior projects or coursework help

## How to Apply
1) Identify labs of interest from NIMHANS departments. 2) Email PI with CV, transcript, SOP, and availability; attach NOC if required. 3) Complete any screening tasks and finalize logistics.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: some labs offer stipends; many certificate-only.
- Remote? Mostly on-site due to clinical/lab work.
- Need ethics training? Yes, follow institute rules and approvals.
