# PGIMER Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
PGIMER hosts research interns in clinical, public health, and biomedical labs. This guide summarizes eligibility, stipend reality, and application steps (Expected/Indicative dates).

## Program Overview
6–8 week on-site projects with department mentors. Work includes data collection, analysis, literature synthesis, or tool development. Certificates standard; stipends vary by lab funding.

## Domains Offered
- Clinical research across specialties
- Public health/epidemiology
- Molecular/biotech labs
- Imaging and health tech analytics

## Eligibility
- Relevant degree track with NOC if needed
- SOP aligned to department; prior research/coursework helps

## How to Apply
1) Identify department/PI. 2) Email with CV, transcript, SOP, and NOC. 3) Respond to tasks/interviews; finalize dates and compliance.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: mostly certificate-only; some funded labs may pay.
- Remote? On-site due to clinical/lab work.
- Ethics approvals? Follow department and IRB rules.
