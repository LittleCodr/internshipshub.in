# AIIMS Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
AIIMS departments host interns across clinical, public health, and basic science labs. This 2026 guide shares eligibility, stipend reality, and application steps (Expected/Indicative dates).

## Program Overview
6–8 week on-site projects with clinicians/researchers. Outputs: data collection/analysis, protocol assistance, literature reviews, and reports. Stipends uncommon; certificates standard.

## Domains Offered
- Clinical research across specialties
- Public health and epidemiology
- Molecular/biotech labs
- Health tech and imaging analytics

## Eligibility
- Relevant degree track and permissions/NOC
- SOP aligned to department; prior research or coursework preferred

## How to Apply
1) Identify target departments/faculty. 2) Email PI with CV, transcript, SOP, and NOC if needed. 3) Complete screenings or tasks; confirm dates and compliance training.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: mostly certificate-only; some funded labs pay stipends.
- Remote? Mostly on-site due to clinical/lab work.
- Need ethics/IC approvals? Yes, follow department/institute rules.
