# SINP Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
SINP offers summer projects across nuclear, high-energy, condensed matter physics, and computation. This guide summarizes 2026 eligibility, stipend, and application steps (Expected/Indicative dates).

## Program Overview
On-site 6–8 week research with faculty supervision, ending in a presentation/report. Stipend usually provided; hostel space limited.

## Domains Offered
- Nuclear and high-energy physics
- Condensed matter and materials
- Astrophysics and cosmology
- Computational/theoretical physics

## Eligibility
- Strong physics/math background; coding or lab skills
- SOP aligned to a faculty group; high CGPA helpful

## How to Apply
1) Check SINP internship call. 2) Identify groups and craft SOP. 3) Submit CV, transcript, and references if asked. 4) Respond quickly to tasks/queries.
