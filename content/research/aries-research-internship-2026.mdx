# ARIES Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
ARIES hosts interns for observational astronomy, atmospheric science, and instrumentation projects in Nainital. This guide outlines 2026 eligibility, stipend, and application steps (Expected/Indicative dates).

## Program Overview
6–8 week on-site projects with telescope data, instrumentation labs, or atmospheric observations. Stipend and hostel support typically provided.

## Domains Offered
- Optical/IR observations and data reduction
- Atmospheric science and space weather
- Instrumentation and control systems

## Eligibility
- Physics/astronomy/EE/CS background; coding or data reduction skills
- SOP aligned to ongoing projects

## How to Apply
1) Check ARIES internship notifications. 2) Draft SOP referencing telescopes/projects. 3) Submit CV, transcripts, and references if asked. 4) Respond quickly to tasks/queries.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: modest stipend; confirm in offer.
- Remote? On-site. 
- Can it extend? Possible with mentor approval for project continuation.
