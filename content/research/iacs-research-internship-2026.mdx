# IACS Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
IACS offers summer research in physics, chemistry, materials, and biology. This guide summarizes eligibility, stipend, and application flow (Expected/Indicative timelines).

## Program Overview
On-site 6–8 week projects with faculty mentors, ending in a report/presentation. Stipend often provided; hostel space limited.

## Domains Offered
- Physical/organic/inorganic chemistry
- Condensed matter and spectroscopy
- Materials and nanoscience
- Biological sciences

## Eligibility
- Senior UG/PG with strong fundamentals
- SOP aligned to a faculty group; transcript and references help

## How to Apply
1) Check IACS internship/call page. 2) Identify faculty groups and craft a focused SOP. 3) Submit CV, transcript, SOP; follow up professionally.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: modest stipend; confirm in offer.
- Remote? On-site only.
- CGPA bar? High CGPA preferred; domain fit matters.
