# Azim Premji University Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
Azim Premji University hosts interns for research in education, development, and public policy. This guide summarizes 2026 eligibility, stipend, and application steps (Expected/Indicative timelines).

## Program Overview
6–10 week internships with faculty/research centers; mix of desk research, data analysis, and fieldwork. Some roles offer stipends or field allowances; others are certificate-only.

## Domains Offered
- Education research and assessments
- Economics of development and livelihoods
- Public policy and governance
- Climate/environment social studies

## Eligibility
- Relevant social science/education background
- Comfort with qual/quant methods; field-readiness if required

## How to Apply
1) Track APU research opportunities page. 2) Apply with CV, transcript, SOP detailing research interests. 3) Share writing samples or data work if requested.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: modest stipend/field allowance; some roles certificate-only.
- Remote? Hybrid possible; field components likely on-site.
- Field travel funded? Check project-specific budgets.
