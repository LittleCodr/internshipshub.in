# NCRA Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
NCRA-TIFR hosts interns for radio astronomy projects tied to GMRT/ORAN data and instrumentation. This guide covers 2026 eligibility, domains, and how to apply (Expected/Indicative timelines).

## Program Overview
6–8 week on-site projects with mentors; work on data pipelines, RFI mitigation, imaging, or hardware. Stipend and housing typically arranged.

## Domains Offered
- Radio astronomy data analysis
- Signal processing and pipelines
- Instrumentation and control
- Software for telescope operations

## Eligibility
- Physics/EE/CS background; coding or DSP skills
- SOP aligned to ongoing projects

## How to Apply
1) Watch NCRA internship calls. 2) Craft SOP referencing GMRT/ORAN work. 3) Submit CV, transcript, and any code repos. 4) Respond to tasks quickly.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: modest stipend with housing support.
- Remote? On-site.
- CGPA bar? High CGPA helps; project fit is decisive.
