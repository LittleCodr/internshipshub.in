## Research focus

Interns support ongoing projects on trustworthy AI, interpretability, and domain generalization for computer vision tasks.

## Mentor pod

You will be paired with a PhD scholar and faculty mentor, receive weekly feedback loops, and contribute to experiments on large-scale datasets.

## Deliverables

- Reproduce baseline models with improved documentation.
- Execute ablation studies and report insights using lab templates.
- Draft a technical blog or poster summarizing findings.

## Application checklist

1. Updated CV with research experience and GitHub links.
2. Statement of Purpose (500 words) outlining interests in trustworthy AI.
3. Letter of recommendation from faculty or industry mentor.
