# MOSPI Research Internship 2026: Eligibility, Stipend, How to Apply, Dates

## Introduction
MOSPI offers internships on statistical systems, surveys, and data analytics. This guide covers 2026 eligibility, stipend, and application steps (Expected/Indicative until official notice).

## Program Overview
8–12 week assignments on survey design, data analysis, dashboards, or documentation. Stipend expected around INR 10k/month; certificate issued on completion.

## Domains Offered
- Survey methodology and sampling
- Economic statistics and national accounts
- Data engineering/visualization for official stats

## Eligibility
- Quantitative degree path; strong stats and coding
- SOP aligned to MOSPI themes

## How to Apply
1) Watch MOSPI internship notification. 2) Submit application with CV, transcript, SOP, and domain preference. 3) Respond to any follow-up or document requests.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: stipend ~INR 10k/month.
- Remote? Typically on-site/hybrid per assignment.
- CGPA bar? Not always stated; strong stats background preferred.
