# Outreachy Remote Internship 2026

## Introduction
Outreachy offers paid, remote internships with open-source organizations to support underrepresented groups in tech. This guide outlines eligibility, timeline, and how to apply. Dates are Expected/Indicative until official calendar posts.

## Program Overview
Interns contribute to production OSS with mentor support. Work is full-time remote over ~12 weeks with milestone evaluations and stipends paid in tranches.

## Domains / Projects Offered
- Web/backend tooling
- Data/ML and scientific software
- Docs, UX, community operations

## Eligibility Criteria
- Must meet Outreachy rules on representation and location
- Demonstrated fit to chosen project; contribution period required

## How to Apply
1) Pick a project and join its communication channel. 2) Make contributions during the contribution period. 3) Submit the application with proposal and contribution evidence. 4) Await mentor review.

## FAQs
- Is it paid? Yes, stipends are paid in regional bands (Expected/Indicative). 
- Fully remote? Yes, entire program is remote. 
- Do I need to be a student? No; career changers allowed per rules.
