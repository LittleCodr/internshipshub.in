# DataKind Remote Data for Good Fellowship 2026

## Introduction
DataKind pairs data teams with nonprofits. This remote fellowship focuses on applied data science for social impact. Dates/stipend are Expected/Indicative.

## Program Overview
- Remote collaboration with DataKind staff and nonprofit partners
- Scoping, modeling, dashboards, and handoff playbooks
- Weekly milestones; certificate and possible stipend

## Eligibility
- Evidence of data work (notebooks, repos, dashboards)
- Comfort with async client communication

## How to Apply
1) Share CV + portfolio via DataKind opportunities. 2) Indicate domain interests (health, education, climate). 3) Complete any take-home or interview.
