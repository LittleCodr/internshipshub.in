# UNICEF MagicBox Remote Data Internship 2026

## Introduction
MagicBox runs open-source analytics for humanitarian response. This remote internship involves data cleaning, dashboards, and models with UNICEF teams. Dates and stipend are Expected/Indicative until posted.

## Program Overview
- Remote collaboration with UNICEF engineers and data scientists
- Focus on mobility, health, and supply-chain datasets
- Milestones with weekly check-ins; certificate and possible stipend

## Eligibility
- Python and SQL proficiency; visualization skills
- Interest in humanitarian data; ability to work async

## How to Apply
1) Prepare CV, portfolio/GitHub, and a short statement of motivation. 2) Watch UNICEF internship postings and submit early. 3) Share sample notebooks or dashboards relevant to mobility/health data.

## FAQs
- Paid? Expected/Indicative: modest stipend or volunteer depending on budget.
- Remote? Yes, fully remote.
- Need prior NGO experience? Not required; data portfolio is key.
